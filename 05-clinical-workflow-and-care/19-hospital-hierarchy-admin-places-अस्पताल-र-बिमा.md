# Section 19: Hospital Hierarchy, Admin & Places (अस्पताल र बिमा)

## 1. Places in the Hospital

- Emergency Room (ER): *Apatkalin kaksha* (आपतकालीन कक्ष) or *Emergency* (इमर्जेन्सी).

- Outpatient Department (OPD): *Bahiranga bibhag* (बहिरंग विभाग) or *OPD* (ओ.पी.डी.).

- Inpatient / Medical Ward: *Ward* (वार्ड).

- Laboratory: *Prayogshala* (प्रयोगशाला) or *Lab* (ल्याब).

- Pharmacy: *Aushadhi pasal* (औषधि पसल) or *Pharmacy* (फार्मेसी).

- Operating Room / Theater: *Salya-kriya kaksha* (शल्यक्रिया कक्ष) or *OT* (ओ.टी.).

- Delivery Room: *Sutkeri kaksha* (सुत्केरी कक्ष).

- Postpartum/Maternity Ward: *Sutkeri ward* (सुत्केरी वार्ड) or *Prasuti ward* (प्रसूति वार्ड).

- Waiting Area: *Pratikshya Kaksha* (प्रतीक्षा कक्ष) (formal) or *Kurne thau* (कुर्ने ठाउँ) (common usage).

## 2. Health System Facilities

- Local Pharmacy/Clinic: *Medical* (मेडिकल - used to describe local privately-owned pharmacies that often do basic triage).

- Health Post: *Swasthya Chauki* (स्वास्थ्य चौकी - rural government clinic).

- Hospital: *Aspatal* (अस्पताल).

- District Hospital: *Zilla Aspatal* (जिल्ला अस्पताल).

## 3. Staff Titles

- Doctor: *Chikitsak* (चिकित्सक - formal) or *Daktar* (डाक्टर - common).

- Nurse: *Paricharika* (परिचारिका - highly formal/literary, rarely used in speech) or *Nurse* (नर्स - common).

- Health Assistant (HA): *HA* (एच.ए. - mid-level provider, often runs health posts).

- CMA / ANM: *CMA* (सी.एम.ए.) or *ANM* (ए.एन.एम. - Auxiliary Nurse Midwife - common in rural areas).

- Female Community Health Volunteer: *Mahila Swasthya Swayamsewika* (महिला स्वास्थ्य स्वयंसेविका) or simply *FCHV* (एफ.सी.एच.भी.).

- Traditional Birth Attendant: *Sudeniy* (सुडेनी).

## 4. Administration, Instructions & Insurance

- Admit to hospital: *Bharna hune* (भर्ना हुने) or *Admit garne*.

- Discharge: *Discharge hune* or *Chhutti hune* (छुट्टी हुने).

- Referral: *Refer garne* or *Arko aspatal ma pathaune*.

- Discharge/OPD Follow-up instruction: *Feri farta aaunu* (फेरि फिर्ता आउनु - come back again) or *Aushadhi sidhiyepachi aaunu* (come back when medicine is finished).

- Health Insurance: *Swasthya Bima* (स्वास्थ्य बिमा) or simply *Bima* (बिमा).

- Insurance Card: *Bima card* (बिमा कार्ड).

- Claiming insurance / Discounted: *Paisa chhut hune* (पैसा छुट हुने - getting a discount/coverage) or *Claim garne*.

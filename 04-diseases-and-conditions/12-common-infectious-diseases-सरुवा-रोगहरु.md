# Section 12: Common Infectious Diseases (सरुवा रोगहरु)

- Common Cold: *Rugha* (रुघा).

- Cough & Cold: *Rugha khoki* (रुघा खोकी).

- Fever: *Jaro* (जरो) or *Jaro aayeko* (having a fever).

- Flu / Viral Fever: *Bhiral jaro* (भाइरल जरो).

- Covid-19: *Covid* (कोभिड) or *Corona* (कोरोना).

- Tuberculosis (TB): *Kshayarog* (क्षयरोग - formal) or simply *TB* (टी.बी. - universal).

- Typhoid: *Taifaad* (टाइफाइड - loanword).

- Dengue: *Dengue* (डेंगु - loanword).

- Malaria: *Malaria* (मलेरिया - widely used loanword) or *Aulo* (औलो - traditional).

- Cholera: *Haija* (हैजा).

- Jaundice / Hepatitis: *Kamalpitta* (कमलपित्त) or *Jaundice* (जन्डिस).

- Rabies: *Rebij* (रेबिज) or *Baula kukur le tokeko rog* (disease from a mad dog bite).

- Leprosy: *Kushtarog* (कुष्ठरोग).

# Section 8: Genitourinary, Pelvic & Sexual History (मुत्र तथा प्रजनन प्रणाली)

See also the [Quick Register Table for Bodily Functions](../01-foundations/part-1-sensitive-language-and-registers.md#quick-register-table-for-bodily-functions) for a quick register comparison, [Section 7: Gastrointestinal & Abdominal](./07-gastrointestinal-abdominal-पाचन-प्रणाली-र-पेट.md) for related elimination vocabulary, and [Section 9: Women's Health, Pregnancy & Childbirth](../03-special-populations/09-womens-health-pregnancy-childbirth-महिला-स्वास्थ्य-र-प्रसूति.md) for reproductive and pregnancy-specific terms.

## 1. Detailed Anatomy

- Kidneys: *Mirgaula* (मिर्गौला)

- Bladder: *Pishab thaili* (पिसाब थैली)

- Private Parts (General): *Guptanga* (गुप्ताङ्ग)

- Anus / Anal opening: *Guda* (गुदा - formal), *Maladwar* (मलद्वार - formal), or *Chaak ko pwaal* (चाकको प्वाल - colloquial).

- Penis: *Linga* (लिङ्ग - formal), *Nunu* (नुनु - peds), *Laado* (लाँडो - taboo).

- Testicles: *Andakosh* (अण्डकोष - formal), *Geda* (गेडा - slang).

- Vagina: *Yoni* (योनी - formal), *Puti* (पुती - taboo/vulgar), or *Bhal* (भाल - taboo/vulgar).

## 2. Urination & Symptoms

- Urine: *Pishab* (पिसाब), *Susu* (सुसु - peds), *Mut* (मुत - taboo/slang).

- Burning urination / Dysuria: *Pishab ferda polne* (पिसाब फेर्दा पोल्ने) or *Pishab poleko* (पिसाब पोलेको).

- UTI / Infected urine: *Pishab pākeko* (पिसाब पाकेको) or *Pishab ko sankraman* (पिसाबको संक्रमण).

- Blood in urine: *Pishab ma ragat aayeko* (पिसाबमा रगत आएको)

- Cloudy urine: *Dhamilo pishab* (धमिलो पिसाब), *Pishab dhamilo bhayeko* (पिसाब धमिलो भएको), or *Dhamilo pishab aayeko* (धमिलो पिसाब आएको)

## 3. Sexual History & Status (यौन इतिहास र अवस्था)

- Married: *Bibahit* (विवाहित) or *Bihe bhayeko* (बिहे भएको).

- Unmarried: *Abibahit* (अविवाहित) or *Bihe nabhayeko* (बिहे नभएको).

- Virgin (Female specifically): *Kumari* (कुमारी) or *Kanya* (कन्या - lit. maiden/unmarried virgin). In some colloquial settings, *Bihe nabhayeko* (बिहे नभएको) is also used to imply sexual inexperience, but it literally means "unmarried" and is not precise enough to rely on clinically.

- Virgin (General/Clinical): *Yaun samparka nagareko* (यौन सम्पर्क नगरेको - has not had sexual intercourse).

- Sexually active: *Yaun samparka ma raheko* (यौन सम्पर्कमा रहेको - formal/clinical) or *Sambhog garne* (सम्भोग गर्ने).

- Sexual Intercourse: *Yaun samparka* (यौन सम्पर्क - clinical), *Sambhog* (सम्भोग - formal), or *Sex garne* (सेक्स गर्ने - common English loanword).

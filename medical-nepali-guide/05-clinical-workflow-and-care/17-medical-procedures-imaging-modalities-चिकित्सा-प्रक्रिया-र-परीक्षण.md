# Section 17: Medical Procedures, Imaging & Modalities (चिकित्सा प्रक्रिया र परीक्षण)

See also [Section 18: Physical Therapy & Rehabilitation](./18-physical-therapy-rehabilitation-भौतिक-चिकित्सा-र-पुनस्थापना.md) for rehabilitation-style movement vocabulary.

## 1. Examinations & General Tests

- Physical Exam: *Jaanch garne* (जाँच गर्ने) or *Parikshan* (परीक्षण - formal test).

- Blood test: *Ragat jhikne* (रगत झिक्ने - draw), *Ragat ko jaanch* (रगतको जाँच), or *Ragat test garne* (रगत टेस्ट गर्ने).

- Urine test: *Pishab ko jaanch* (पिसाबको जाँच).

- Stool test: *Disa ko jaanch* (दिसाको जाँच).

## 2. Physical Exam Commands (शारीरिक परीक्षणमा प्रयोग हुने निर्देशनहरु)

These are usually given in respectful imperative form. In real clinical speech, many clinicians soften them further with *-dinuhos* (दिनुहोस्) phrasing, especially with older patients or during sensitive exams.

**Positioning and moving on the bed / exam table**

- Lie down: *Sutnuhos* (सुत्नुहोस्).

- Lie on your back / lie flat: *Sidhha sutnuhos* (सिधा सुत्नुहोस्) or *Pithyu ko bharma sutnuhos* (पिठ्युँको भरमा सुत्नुहोस्).

- Lie face down / lie prone: *Ghopto parnuhos* (घोप्टो पर्नुहोस्) or *Pet ko bharma sutnuhos* (पेटको भरमा सुत्नुहोस्).

- Lie on your side: *Kolto parnuhos* (कोल्टो पर्नुहोस्).

- Roll this way: *Yeta tira farkinuhos* (यता तिर फर्किनुहोस्) or *Yeta kolto parnuhos* (यता कोल्टो पर्नुहोस् - while lying).

- Roll that way: *Uta tira farkinuhos* (उता तिर फर्किनुहोस्) or *Uta kolto parnuhos* (उता कोल्टो पर्नुहोस् - while lying).

- Turn over / flip over completely: *Paltinuhos* (पल्टिनुहोस्) or *Farkinuhos* (फर्किनुहोस्). For specificity, you can say *Ghopto bata sidhha parnuhos* (घोप्टोबाट सिधा पर्नुहोस् - turn from prone to supine) or *Sidhha bata ghopto parnuhos* (सिधाबाट घोप्टो पर्नुहोस् - turn from supine to prone).

- Sit down: *Basnuhos* (बस्नुहोस्).

- Sit up (from lying): *Uthera basnuhos* (उठेर बस्नुहोस्).

- Stand up: *Ubhinuhos* (उभिनुहोस्).

- Bend forward / bend over: *Agadi tira jhuknuhos* (अगाडि तिर झुक्नुहोस्).

- Squat down: *Ghunda modera tala basnuhos* (घुँडा मोडेर तल बस्नुहोस्).

- Turn around: *Pachhadi farkinuhos* (पछाडि फर्किनुहोस्).

- Walk a little: *Ali hidnuhos* (अलि हिँड्नुहोस्).

- Stand on one foot: *Euta khuttama ubhinuhos* (एउटा खुट्टामा उभिनुहोस्).

- Hop: *Phadkinuhos* (फड्किनुहोस्) or *Euta khuttale phadkinuhos* (एउटा खुट्टाले फड्किनुहोस्).

**Breathing, relaxation, and general cooperation**

- Take a deep breath: *Thulo saas linnuhos* (ठूलो सास लिनुहोस् - very natural bedside phrasing), *Gahiro saas linnuhos* (गहिरो सास लिनुहोस् - written/common), or *Lamo saas linnuhos* (लामो सास लिनुहोस् - also common).

- Inhale: *Saas tannuhos* (सास तान्नुहोस्).

- Hold your breath: *Saas rokhnuhos* (सास रोक्नुहोस्).

- Exhale / breathe out: *Saas chodnuhos* (सास छोड्नुहोस्).

- Exhale all your breath forcefully: *Saas sabai jor le chodnuhos* (सास सबै जोरले छोड्नुहोस्).

- Relax: *Relax garnuhos* (रिल्याक्स गर्नुहोस्) or *Khukulo hunuhos* (खुकुलो हुनुहोस्).

- Breathe normally / stop holding your breath: *Sadhai jastai saas linnuhos* (सधैं जस्तै सास लिनुहोस्).

- Don't worry: *Chinta nagarnuhos* (चिन्ता नगर्नुहोस्) or *Darlagnu pardaina* (डर लाग्नु पर्दैन).

- Keep still: *Nachalnuhos* (नचल्नुहोस्) or *Sthir basnuhos* (स्थिर बस्नुहोस्).

- Don't talk: *Nabolnuhos* (नबोल्नुहोस्).

- Tell me if it hurts: *Dukhe bhane bhannuhos* (दुखे भने भन्नुहोस्).

**Eyes, head, face, and mouth**

- Left / right note: For bedside directions, you will commonly hear *Debre* (देब्रे - everyday/common "left") and *Daaya* (दायाँ - standard/common "right"). The more symmetrical written pair is *Baaya* (बायाँ - left) and *Daaya* (दायाँ - right). In fast speech, *Baaya/Daaya* may sound to learners more like *Biya/Diya*, so it is worth recognizing both the written forms and the spoken variants. If a patient does not follow one pair, try the other.

- Open your eyes: *Aankha kholnuhos* (आँखा खोल्नुहोस्).

- Close your eyes: *Aankha banda garnuhos* (आँखा बन्द गर्नुहोस्).

- Look straight ahead: *Sidhha hernuhos* (सिधा हेर्नुहोस्).

- Look left: *Debre tira hernuhos* (देब्रे तिर हेर्नुहोस्) or *Baaya/Biya tira hernuhos* (बायाँ तिर हेर्नुहोस्).

- Look right: *Daaya/Diya tira hernuhos* (दायाँ तिर हेर्नुहोस्).

- Look up: *Mathi hernuhos* (माथि हेर्नुहोस्).

- Look down: *Tala hernuhos* (तल हेर्नुहोस्).

- Turn your head to the left / right: *Tauko debre / daaya farkaunuhos* (टाउको देब्रे / दायाँ फर्काउनुहोस्) or *Tauko baaya / daaya farkaunuhos* (टाउको बायाँ / दायाँ फर्काउनुहोस्). In fast speech this may sound closer to *Tauko biya / diya farkaunuhos*.

- Follow my finger: *Mero aunlaa herirahnuhos* (मेरो औंला हेरिरहनुहोस्).

- Open your mouth: *Mukh kholnuhos* (मुख खोल्नुहोस्).

- Say "ahhh": *Aa... bhannuhos* (आ... भन्नुहोस्).

- Stick out your tongue: *Jibro bahira nikaalnuhos* (जिब्रो बाहिर निकाल्नुहोस्).

- Swallow: *Nilnuhos* (निल्नुहोस्).

- Cough: *Khoknuhos* (खोक्नुहोस्) or *Jor le khoknuhos* (जोरले खोक्नुहोस्).

- Spit / spit it out: *Thuknuhos* (थुक्नुहोस्), *Yahaa thuknuhos* (यहाँ थुक्नुहोस्), or *Khakaar faalnuhos* (खकार फाल्नुहोस् - especially for sputum/phlegm).

- Smile: *Haasnuhos* (हाँस्नुहोस्).

- Show your teeth: *Daant dekhaunuhos* (दाँत देखाउनुहोस्).

- Raise your eyebrows: *Bhau mathi uthaunuhos* (भौं माथि उठाउनुहोस्).

**Arms, legs, strength, and gait testing**

- Raise your arms: *Haat mathi uthaunuhos* (हात माथि उठाउनुहोस्) or *Dubaai haat mathi uthaunuhos* (दुवै हात माथि उठाउनुहोस्).

- Lower your arms: *Haat tala lyaunuhos* (हात तल ल्याउनुहोस्).

- Lift your leg: *Khutta mathi uthaunuhos* (खुट्टा माथि उठाउनुहोस्).

- Lower your leg: *Khutta tala lyaunuhos* (खुट्टा तल ल्याउनुहोस्).

- Squeeze my fingers / hand: *Mero aunlaa chyapnuhos* (मेरो औंला च्याप्नुहोस्) or *Mero haat samaatnuhos* (मेरो हात समात्नुहोस्).

- Push against my hand: *Mero haatma jor lagaunuhos* (मेरो हातमा जोर लगाउनुहोस्) or *Mero haat tira dhakelnuhos* (मेरो हात तिर धकेल्नुहोस्).

- Pull toward you: *Aafutira taannuhos* (आफूतिर तान्नुहोस्).

- Shrug your shoulders: *Kaandh mathi uthaunuhos* (काँध माथि उठाउनुहोस्).

- Touch your nose: *Naak chhunuhos* (नाक छुनुहोस्).

- Walk heel-to-toe: *Euta khuttako agadi arko khutta raakhera hidnuhos* (एउटा खुट्टाको अगाडि अर्को खुट्टा राखेर हिँड्नुहोस्).

**When the clinician needs passive movement**

- Let me move it: *Malai chalauna dinuhos* (मलाई चलाउन दिनुहोस्).

- Let me do the work: *Tapai relax garnuhos, ma chalauchhu* (तपाईं रिल्याक्स गर्नुहोस्, म चलाउँछु).

- Don't help / don't resist: *Bal nalagaunuhos* (बल नलगाउनुहोस्).

- Keep it loose / relaxed: *Khukulo chhodnuhos* (खुकुलो छोड्नुहोस्).

## 3. Imaging Vocabulary

- X-Ray: *X-ray garne* (एक्स-रे गर्ने).

- Ultrasound: *Bhitri photo khichne* (भित्री फोटो खिच्ने) or *Video X-ray* (भिडियो एक्स-रे - extremely common term in Nepal for USG).

- CT Scan: *CT Scan* (सिटी स्क्यान).

- MRI: *MRI* (एम.आर.आई.).

## 4. Describing Image Findings to Patients

- Spot / Shadow seen (often used for lung/TB findings): *Daag dekhiyo* (दाग देखियो).

- Fluid accumulation (effusion/edema): *Pani jameko chha* (पानी जमेको छ).

- Cavity / Hole (TB/abscess): *Khoyalto* (खोयाल्टो).

- Swelling seen inside: *Bhitra sunniyeko dekhiyo* (भित्र सुन्निएको देखियो).

- Pinched nerve (on MRI): *Nasa chyapiyeko* (नशा च्यापिएको).

- Prenatal Ultrasound - Baby's heartbeat: *Bacha ko mutu ko chal* (बच्चाको मुटुको चाल).

- Prenatal Ultrasound - Baby moving: *Bacha chaleko* (बच्चा चलेको).

## 5. Specialized Treatment Modalities

- Surgery / Operation: *Salya-kriya* (शल्यक्रिया), *Operation* (अपरेशन), or *Chir-faar* (चिरफार - lit. cutting and opening).

- Robotic Surgery: *Robot bata operation* (रोबोटबाट अपरेशन) or *Robotic salya-kriya* (रोबोटिक शल्यक्रिया).

- Dialysis: *Dialysis garne* (डायलाइसिस गर्ने).

- Sitz Bath: *Tato pani ma basne* (तातो पानीमा बस्ने - lit. sitting in hot water) or *Sitz bath* (सिट्स बाथ).

- Light Therapy (e.g., phototherapy for neonatal jaundice): *Batti ma rakhne* (बत्तीमा राख्ने - lit. keeping under the light) or *Phototherapy* (फोटोथेरापी). Avoid *sekne* here, as it implies adding heat rather than light.

- Laser Therapy (e.g., for skin treatments): *Laser upachaar* (लेजर उपचार) or *Laser therapy* (लेजर थेरापी).
